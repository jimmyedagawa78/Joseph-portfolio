About Me

JOSEPH
BLESSING
STEPHEN

I'm a video editor, graphic designer, and content creator based in Tiruchirapalli, India — with a background that spans digital marketing, community management, and brand storytelling.

My work lives at the intersection of visuals and strategy. Whether it's a cinematic edit, a scroll-stopping social post, or a logo built from scratch, I approach every project with the same question: what story does this need to tell?

I've built and managed a community of 12,000+ members, coordinated partnerships with cultural institutions and cinema brands, and delivered creative work for international clients through Fiverr and independent engagements. I've also contributed to the global rollout of a UK SaaS product across the US and EU markets.

I hold a Master of Commerce from Bishop Heber College, where I graduated with an 84% score and also served as the digital designer for major college events.
