First Look at Resident Evil Live-action Cast

By Joseph Blessing Stephen · 1 min read · 2021

Not too long ago we got an animated series starring the Resident Evil crew on Netflix — and now we have received our first look at Claire, Leon, and Jill Valentine in the new live-action adaptation: Resident Evil: Welcome to Raccoon City.

Claire Redfield and the rest of the cast, as well as Umbrella test subject Lisa Trevor, are featured in new first-look photographs from the upcoming reboot directed by Johannes Roberts.

WHAT WE'VE SEEN

The images lean heavily into the gritty, grounded aesthetic that the original games are known for. Unlike the Paul W.S. Anderson film series — which took significant liberties with the source material — Welcome to Raccoon City appears to be a more faithful adaptation, drawing directly from the events of the first two games.

For fans of the games, the casting and visual direction give genuine reasons to be hopeful.

The Raccoon City setting looks suitably dark and oppressive, and the costumes appear to be close to the game designs. Whether the film can capture the tension and atmosphere of the source material remains to be seen — but the first look is promising.
